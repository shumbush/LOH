OUR STORY

OUR STORY

Founded in 2006, The Network of Lawyers of Hope in Rwanda (LOH) is an association of Christian Lawyers legally operating in Rwanda as a Non-Governmental Organization with the Legal Personality no95/08.11.LOH is among the Rwandan NGOs that work on the protection and promotion of human rights, and it is committed to defend the cause of the poor and needy, and to reach out to the most vulnerable members of the Rwandan society; in particular Children, Women and other needy people to seek justice and wellbeing on their behalf and share with them Christ love and compassion. We work to promote social justice and sustainable development.
