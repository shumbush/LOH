Child
Protection

LOH works towards the protection and promotion of children rights to minimize the risks of any form of violence against them. We work by empowering communities, local leaders and children themselves to help prevent and respond to any abuse, exploitation, neglect and any other variable form of violence affecting the development and well-being of children.

Legal
Education

To promote equitable access to justice for all, LOH provides legal aid to vulnerable members of communities through legal representation in courts and community outreach on rights, laws and policies.

Gender
Promotion

To help prevent and stop all forms of violence against women and girls, LOH works to advance gender equity, equality, and the equitable enjoyment of rights by men and women.

We improve efforts to prevent violence against women and girls and promote sexual reproductive health and rights education to our target populations.

Women
Empowerment

LOH empowers and strengthens women to unlock their full potential by providing financial education, vocational training, helping them access financial capital, grouping them into business cooperatives and helping them access the market.

Youth Empowerment

LOH envisions to be the hub for youth empowerment and talent development by creating a space for life skills development where young people meet to learn various valuable 21st century employability skills in the marketplace, plus acquiring and practicing leadership skills.

We are an association of Christian lawyers working in Rwanda as a local non-governmental organization since 2006 on the promotion and protection of human rights.
