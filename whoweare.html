Wo we are

Our Story

Founded in 2006,The Network of Lawyers of Hope in Rwanda (LOH) is an association of Christian Lawyers legally operating in Rwanda as a Non-Governmental Organization with the Legal Personality no95/08.11.

LOH is among the Rwandan NGOs that work on the protection and promotion of human rights, and it is committed to defend the cause of the poor and needy, and to reach out to the most vulnerable members of the Rwandan society; in particular Children, Women and other needy people to seek justice and wellbeing on their behalf and share with them Christ love and compassion.We work to promote social justice and sustainable development.

Contact Us

Our Misson

Our mission is to defend the rights of the poor and needy (Proverbs 31:9) by being a hub that enables lawyers and non-lawyers, stakeholders to contribute to access to justice and improved well-being of vulnerable members of our communities.

Our Vision

Our vision is to defend the cause of the poor and needy.
"As an association of Christian lawyers, we seek to reach out to the most vulnerable members of our communities and society at large, in particular children, women, prisoners and other needy people; to seek justice on their behalf, contribute to their welfare, and share with them Christ's love and compassion."

CORE VALUES

Christian & Compasion

We strive to demonstrate the highest standard of speiritual, ethical and professional integrity and pratice in our work. our work goes beyond mere professional output and extends to the realm of ministry

Accountability

We are Accountable to the communities we serve, the government, our partners and stakeholders.

Compliance

We are compliant to government rules and regulations our partners and stakeholders.

Equity & Equality

We are mindful of the negative impacts that history, culture and poverty can have upon rights. Our strongest belief is that all men and women are made equal in God's sight; and that kingdom identity takes primacy over other identities. We guarantee equity and equality in all of our programs

Justice

We believe that access to justice is a fundamental human right, and so we strive to ensure that members of our communities have access to justice, and we ensure that all our staff and those involved in our activities be treated fairly

We are an association of Christian lawyers working in Rwanda as a local non-governmental organization since 2006 on the promotion and protection of human rights.
